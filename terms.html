2. Description of Service

Focus For Good is a productivity application that helps you stay focused by:

  • Blocking distracting apps during focus sessions using Apple's Screen Time API

Operating Modes

Serious Mode: You commit credits to your focus session. Complete the session to keep your credits. End early and credits are forfeited.

Lite Mode: Free to use. Watch an unskippable advertisement to end your session early.
